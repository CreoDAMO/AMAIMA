  - **Option 3 (Commercial License)**: Paid, unrestricted—removes all bans (e.g., on competition, AI training), adds benefits like support/indemnification. This is your primary monetization lever for enterprises.
- **Protections to Consider**: 
  - **Novel Elements Safeguards**: Monetization can't enable unauthorized replication (e.g., no selling derivatives that compete with AMAIMA's core innovations).
  - **Ethical/Prohibited Uses**: All strategies must enforce Part IX (e.g., no monetizing for harmful apps like surveillance or discrimination).

- **Opportunities**: The license encourages community growth (Option 1) to funnel users to paid tiers (Option 3), with built-in upsell paths (e.g., notify free users at 1,000 MAU limit).
